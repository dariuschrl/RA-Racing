My Story

The Journey So Far

Darius Chirila

Professional Race Car Driver

I come from Romania, a country where racing is not as appreciated as it should be, but I am determined to change that, and become a professional race car driver, with the help of RA Racing, a club that I am a part of.

The Beginning

Ever since I was a kid, I dreamed of driving fast cars. Last year, I took the plunge and signed up for a race car driving course. The experience was mind-blowing, and I realized I had found my calling.

BRSCC Fiesta Junior Scholarship

At the beginning of 2023, I took part in the BRSCC Fiesta Junior Scholarship in the UK. Despite not winning it, I learned a lot and became a better driver, understanding the world of motorsport internationally.

First Race Victory

My first race was in March 2023 at Motorpark Romania. In my last race of the season, after 8 hours of hard racing through tough conditions, I managed to secure first place in my class.

Next Step: Win in the BMW Challenge

That next step is winning a race in the BMW Challenge, an international series popular among amateur drivers worldwide. For this, I will need to train even harder and perfect my racecraft.

Ultimate Goal

Now, I'm looking ahead, with the World Endurance Championship as my ultimate goal. It won't be easy, but with each race, I'll be a step closer to making my dreams reality.
