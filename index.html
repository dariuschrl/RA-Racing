My Story

The Journey So Far

I come from a rather small city in Romania where the only way to ride a car as a minor is on the single track that's available in the country, Motorpark.
Ever since I was a kid, I dreamed of becoming a race car driver, and things are starting to become closer to reality.


As time passed, my dream to race professionally never faded. But there were moments of uncertainty, times when I couldn’t see the full picture. Then, I came across a quote from The Boy, the Mole, the Fox and the Horse by Charlie Mackesy, which resonated with me:

"I can't see a way through," said the boy.
"Can you see your next step?"
"Yes."
"Just take that," said the horse.

It made me realize I didn’t need to have it all figured out. I just needed to focus on the next step. That next step is entering the Clio Cup, a big challenge but one I know would bring me closer to my dream.
Now, I’m looking ahead, with the World Endurance Championship as my ultimate goal. It won’t be easy, but with each race, I’ll be a step closer. As the horse wisely said, I just have to keep moving forward, one step at a time.
